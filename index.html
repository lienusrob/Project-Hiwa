About Us

Project Hiwa is a sports center run by asylum seekers and open to all. A rare places in Athens offering sports for free. It started around the summer of 2019. and since that moment it has become an important place to go for many children, women and men, regardless of origin, religion and language.

The project is co founded by Dana and Kamara, 2 athletes that were also invÎ¿lved in the start of Yoga and Sport for Refugees on Lesvos. In September 2020 Yoga and Sport for Refugees was approached take over the project and have it under their umbrella.

Project Hiwa belongs to the Non Profit organsiation Yoga and Sports for Refugeeswhich is located on the greek Island Lesvos

Our values

Interculturarity

In Yoga and Sport/ Project Hiwa for refugees there is people from all over the world, there is a mixing of cultures, religions and languages. We want to establish a link between cultures through sport so that everyone feels integrated and part of the community.

No Borders

Doing sport with people from all over the world we cross the limits of national borders. When we do sport we have partners and rivals, we share equipment and objectives; the important thing to achieve the challenge, everything else loses importance.

No Limitations

At Project Hiwa we do not want anyone to set limits when we talk about sports, we want everyone to maximize theirself, always move forward and never surrender. We understand competition and effort as values for self-esteem, which dignify the individual and give an objective during the forced stay.

Empowerment

The situation in which refugees in Lesbos and Athens are currently living is shameful and denigrating. We want them to regain dignity, participate in the community and build trust in their own capacities, recovering responsibilities, rights and duties.

Activities

  • At Project Hiwa and Yoga and Sports we count more than 108 hours of sport classes per week split in different activities. The median number of student per class are for martial arts (25-30), field-sports (20-25), running (10) and yoga (10-15). The trainings are alocate to different area in Athens as for example the YSR Gym where martial arts and dancing happen. In 2019, we attended 9 special running competion.

  • We develop our activity around two main sports: Yoga (Relax Yoga, Vinyasa yoga, Akroyoga or Thai-yoga massage) and Martial Arts (Wrestling, Kickboxing, Muy Thai, Jiu-Jitsu). Additionaly, we are conducting swimming classes, fitness sessions(body building, HIIT training, Weightlifting, Gymnasistcs-parkour), running sessions and dancing classes (Afro-house, Breakdance).

  • Therefore, to privilege womens we have adapted varied classes only available for them such as Yoga, Zumba, Hip-Hop, Empowerment-self and defence. This allows refugees women to socialise and get involve without difficulties or shyness. Today, Project Hiwa is implement in Athens and Levos. Our aim is to develop our community all around the refugees camps as shown on the map above.
